Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines can vary greatly depending on the project's scope and complexity. A simple static website might take a few weeks, while a more complex web application with custom features could take several months. I work closely with clients to establish a clear project plan and provide regular updates to ensure a smooth and efficient process.

Do you offer ongoing support after a project is completed?

Yes, I provide ongoing maintenance and support to ensure your website or application remains secure, up-to-date, and fully functional. This can include bug fixes, feature enhancements, and general technical assistance. We can discuss a tailored support plan that fits your specific needs.

Can you work on a project from scratch, or do you only do maintenance?

I can do both! I enjoy building new applications from the ground up, starting with concept and design and moving all the way to deployment. I am also happy to step in and provide maintenance, updates, or new feature development for an existing project.

How do you handle project communication and updates?

I believe in clear and consistent communication. I typically use a combination of email, instant messaging, and scheduled video calls to keep clients informed. I provide regular progress updates and am always available to answer any questions you may have throughout the project.
